## üí° CONTENT BEST PRACTICES

### Writing Style:
- Professional but accessible
- Avoid excessive legal jargon
- Include real-world examples
- Use bullet points and numbered lists
- Break up text with subheadings

### SEO Optimization:
- Primary keyword in H1
- Secondary keywords in H2s
- Natural keyword integration (2-3% density)
- Long-tail keyword variations
- LSI keywords throughout

### Engagement:
- Start with compelling introduction
- Answer the question in first paragraph
- Include table of contents for long articles
- Add conclusion with key takeaways
- Clear CTA at the end

### Trust Signals:
- Cite relevant laws and acts
- Reference recent court cases
- Include expert opinions
- Add disclaimer (general information, not legal advice)
- Author attribution with credentials
